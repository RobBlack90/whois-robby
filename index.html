What I Do

I’m a full stack developer- I full stack develop! I wasn’t born that way, obviously. Although the idea of a programming baby is both adorable and possibly box office gold... I was just born with a love for solving problems- software has become my chosen medium to do so. Being versed in both server and client side development means being able to provide more well rounded solutions.

Early in my development career I realized that it doesn't matter how useful your app is- if it looks bad, nobody's going to use it. That simple realization started my journey down the UI/UX rabbit hole. I'm always trying to learn how to better create simple, usable designs that "gets out of the user's way."

What I Do

I’m a full stack developer! ...I full stack develop! Well, obviously, I wasn’t born a developer. Although a programming baby would be both adorable and possibly box office gold... I was born with a passion for solving problems- software has just become my chosen medium to do so. Being versed in both server and client side development means being able to provide more well rounded solutions to any given problem.

Early in my development career I realized that it doesn't matter how useful your app is- if it looks bad, nobody's going to use it. That meant diving straight into the deep end of the colorful, perfectly margined, CSS Grid aligned UI/UX swimming pool. Learning different methods to create simple, useful designs has quickly become one of my favorite aspects of the entire development process.
